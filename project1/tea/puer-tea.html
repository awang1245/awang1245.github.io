Pu'er Tea

普洱茶 [pǔ'ěr chá]

Pu-erh is an aged and fermented tea from Yunnan, China, often pressed into cakes. It uses large-leaf tea varieties and develops deeper flavor with time. There are two types: sheng (raw) and shou (ripe).

Sheng can be fresh and bright when young, aging into smoother, sweet herbal notes. Shou is rich, earthy, and smooth, with hints like wood, cocoa, or dried fruit. Both types are good for multiple infusions.
