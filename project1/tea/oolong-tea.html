Oolong Tea

乌龙茶 [wū lóng chá]

Oolong tea sits between green and black tea, made through partial oxidation and skilled rolling or shaping. It originated in Fujian and Taiwan and comes in many styles, from very floral to deeper roasted flavors. Oolong is known for smooth texture, layered aroma, and long-lasting aftertaste.

Light oolongs are floral and creamy, while darker ones bring toastier, caramel-like notes. Many oolongs perform best over multiple short infusions. They are popular in gongfu-style brewing.
